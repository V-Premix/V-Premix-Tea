Our Journey

Our Story

Every great idea begins with a simple problem.

At V-Primix Tea, we noticed that millions of people wanted a good cup of chai, but didn't always have the time, milk, sugar, or equipment to prepare it. Whether at work, while travelling, or during a busy day, making traditional tea wasn't always practical.

That challenge inspired us to create something different.

We developed V-Primix Tea—a premium all-in-one tea premix that delivers the authentic taste of Indian chai in just one minute. Simply add hot water, stir, and enjoy. Every sachet is carefully crafted with quality tea, milk powder, sugar, and selected flavours to provide a rich and consistent tea experience.

Our mission is simple: to make great chai available anytime, anywhere, without compromising on taste or quality.

At V-Primix, we don't just sell tea—we create convenience, comfort, and a memorable chai experience in every cup. As we continue to grow, our vision is to make V-Primix a trusted name in homes, offices, travel, and businesses across India and beyond.
