Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, ordering process, and partnership.

About Our Products

We currently offer 5 premium variants: Masala Tea, Ginger Tea, Elaichi (Cardamom) Tea, Green Tea, and Lemon Tea premixes. All products are FSSAI certified and available in multiple packaging sizes. We also offer custom formulations for enterprise clients.

Yes, all V-Premix Tea products carry a valid FSSAI license (11524999000XXX). Our manufacturing facility is also ISO 9001:2015 certified. We maintain complete batch traceability from raw material sourcing to dispatch.

Our premixes have a shelf life of 12 months from the date of manufacturing when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Triple-layer moisture-proof packaging ensures product integrity throughout the shelf life.

Preparation is extremely simple: Add 14g (approximately 1 tablespoon) of V-Premix Tea powder to 100ml of hot water (85–90°C). Stir well for 30 seconds until dissolved. Serve hot. No milk, sugar, or extra ingredients are needed — it's all in the blend.

No. V-Premix Tea products are made with real tea extracts, authentic spices, and natural ingredients only. We never use artificial colors, synthetic flavors, or chemical preservatives. Our commitment to authenticity is what sets us apart.

Ordering & Pricing

Our minimum order quantity is 10 kg for standard orders. However, for first-time trial orders, we offer a reduced MOQ of 2 kg at standard pricing so you can test the product before committing to bulk.

You can place an order by: (1) Filling the inquiry form on our Contact page, (2) WhatsApp us directly at +91 63523 69851, or (3) Email us at sales@vpremixtea.com. A dedicated account manager will process your order within 24 business hours.

For new clients, we require 100% advance payment. After the first 3 successful orders, established clients are eligible for a 30-day credit period based on order volume and business profile. Please discuss terms with your account manager.

Yes, we have a tiered pricing structure. Orders above 50kg receive a 5% discount, above 200kg receive 10%, and above 500kg receive 15%. Dealers enrolled in our network program receive additional exclusive pricing. Contact us for a custom quote.

Delivery & Logistics

Yes, we deliver to all major cities and pin codes across India through our partnership with leading logistics providers. Estimated delivery time is 3–7 business days for most locations. Remote areas may take up to 10 business days.

Orders above 25 kg qualify for free delivery to most metro cities. For smaller orders or remote locations, delivery charges are calculated based on weight and destination and communicated before order confirmation.

We take packaging damage very seriously. If your order arrives damaged, please photograph the packaging and contents and WhatsApp us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement shipment at no extra cost or issue a full refund.
