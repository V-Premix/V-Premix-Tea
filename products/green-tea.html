Product Overview

V-Premix Green Tea is crafted from premium green tea extract, delivering the full spectrum of natural antioxidants and polyphenols. Unlike black tea premixes, our green tea has a lighter, more delicate profile with a subtle sweetness and refreshing finish.

As consumers increasingly prioritize wellness, green tea has become a must-have on menus. Our premix format makes it easy to consistently serve premium quality green tea at high volume.

How to Prepare

1
Measure — Take 12g (slightly less than 1 tablespoon) of V-Premix Green Tea.
2
Heat Water — Use water at 75–80°C (not boiling — preserves antioxidants).
3
Mix — Stir premix into hot water gently for 20 seconds.
4
Serve — Serve in a glass cup to showcase the beautiful golden-green color.
